About Reports

Use these reports to gain insights into your time tracking patterns and productivity:

  • Task Report: Shows how much time you've spent on each task, comparing actual vs. estimated time.
  • Customer Report: Breaks down time allocation by customer, helping you understand which clients require the most attention.
  • Project Report: Provides project-level time tracking to help with project management and billing.
  • Time Entries: A chronological log of all your time tracking activities with detailed information.
Tip: Regularly review these reports to optimize your time management and ensure accurate project billing.
